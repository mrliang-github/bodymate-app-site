Privacy Policy

BodyMate is designed as an on-device personal record. The current version does not require an account, include advertising, track users across apps or websites, or use third-party analytics.

Data you enter

Body measurements, training records, goals, and profile settings that you enter are stored locally in BodyMate on your device. Current record and trend features do not send this information to the developer's servers.

Apple Health

Apple Health access is optional and controlled by your system permissions. When enabled, BodyMate reads only the data categories you authorize so it can display activity, sleep, and related health context in the app. BodyMate does not use Apple Health data for advertising, profiling, or sale.

Bluetooth devices

Bluetooth access is optional and used to connect to supported body scales and read a measurement you initiate. Measurements are stored with your BodyMate records on the device.

Exports and sharing

If you choose an export or share action, iOS presents the system share sheet. You decide the destination. The destination service's privacy terms apply after you choose to send a file there.

Data collection and tracking

The current version does not include advertising SDKs, third-party analytics, cross-app tracking, or a developer-operated account system. BodyMate does not sell personal information.

Retention and deletion

Local records remain on your device until you delete them or remove the app. Apple Health data remains under your control in Apple Health. Revoking Apple Health or Bluetooth permission stops future access.

Children

BodyMate is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children through a developer-operated service.

Changes and contact

If a future release changes how data is handled, this policy and the App Store privacy disclosure will be updated before that release. For privacy questions, email mrliang8816@gmail.com.

隐私政策

BodyMate 按照“本机优先”的方式设计。当前版本无需账号，不包含广告，不进行跨 App 或网站跟踪，也不使用第三方分析服务。

你输入的数据

你输入的身体数据、训练记录、目标和个人设置保存在设备上的 BodyMate 中。当前记录与趋势功能不会把这些信息发送到开发者服务器。

Apple 健康

Apple 健康是可选功能，并由系统权限控制。启用后，BodyMate 只读取你授权的数据类型，用于在 App 内展示活动、睡眠和相关健康信息。BodyMate 不会把 Apple 健康数据用于广告、用户画像或出售。

蓝牙设备

蓝牙是可选功能，用于连接受支持的体脂秤并读取由你发起的测量。测量结果与 BodyMate 记录一起保存在设备上。

导出与分享

当你主动选择导出或分享时，iOS 会显示系统分享面板，目标位置由你决定。文件发送到所选服务后，将适用该服务的隐私规则。

数据收集与跟踪

当前版本不包含广告 SDK、第三方分析、跨 App 跟踪或开发者运营的账号系统。BodyMate 不出售个人信息。

保留与删除

本地记录会保留在设备上，直到你删除记录或移除 App。Apple 健康数据仍由你在 Apple 健康中管理。撤销 Apple 健康或蓝牙权限后，BodyMate 将停止后续访问。

儿童隐私

BodyMate 不以儿童为目标，也不会通过开发者运营的服务主动收集儿童个人信息。

政策更新与联系

如果未来版本改变数据处理方式，我们会在发布前更新本政策与 App Store 隐私披露。如有隐私问题，请发送邮件至 mrliang8816@gmail.com。
