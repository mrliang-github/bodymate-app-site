Frequently asked questions

Can I use BodyMate without a scale?

Yes. Open Record and choose Body Data to enter weight and body fat manually. Bluetooth scale support is optional and limited to compatible devices.

How do I record training?

Open Record and choose Training. Exercises can use duration, sets and total reps, or total reps depending on the exercise.

Is Apple Health required?

No. Apple Health access is optional. If enabled, BodyMate reads only the categories you authorize and displays them inside the app.

How do I remove my records?

You can remove manually created entries in the app. Deleting BodyMate from the device removes its local app data. Apple Health data remains managed by Apple Health.

BodyMate is a personal record and trend-viewing tool. It does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or emergency monitoring.

常见问题

没有体脂秤也能使用吗？

可以。在“记录”中选择“身体数据”，即可手动填写体重与体脂。蓝牙体脂秤是可选功能，仅限兼容设备。

如何记录训练？

在“记录”中选择“训练”。不同动作可按时长、组数与总次数，或总次数进行记录。

必须连接 Apple 健康吗？

不需要。Apple 健康为可选功能；启用后，BodyMate 只读取你明确授权的数据类型并在 App 内展示。

如何删除记录？

你可以在 App 内删除手动创建的记录。删除 BodyMate 会移除它保存在设备上的本地数据；Apple 健康中的数据仍由 Apple 健康管理。

BodyMate 用于个人记录与趋势查看，不提供医疗诊断、治疗建议或紧急监测。
